# 📊 Data Visualization Tools: Chart Selection for Superstore Insights

This project focuses on applying **data visualization best practices** to answer 30 scenario-based business questions using the **Sample Superstore Dataset**. The objective is to choose the most appropriate chart type for each scenario and extract actionable insights using **Tableau** and **other visualization tools**.



## 🎯 Objective

- Select the best chart type based on question context.
- Extract business insights and support decision-making.
- Improve proficiency in choosing the right visualization tools.
- Build a reference guide for dashboard/report design.

---

## 📊 Visualization Techniques Covered

| Chart Type                 | Use Case Highlights |
|---------------------------|---------------------|
| **Bar Chart**             | Compare categorical values like sales by region or shipping modes |
| **Line Chart**            | Track trends over time like monthly sales or quarterly growth |
| **Pie Chart**             | Show proportion or contribution of categories like sales share by region |
| **Stacked/Side-by-Side Bar** | Compare groups and sub-groups (e.g., profit by segment & category) |
| **Heatmap**               | Regional sales intensity comparisons |
| **Scatter Plot**          | Identify relationships between metrics like discounts vs. sales |
| **Histogram**             | Visualize distribution of order quantities |
| **Maps**                  | Geographical distribution of returns and profit impact |
| **Dual Axis / Dual Line Charts** | Compare trends like sales vs profit simultaneously |

---

## 🧠 Key Business Questions Answered (Sample)

1. **Which product categories have the highest sales?** → *Bar Chart*
2. **How do monthly sales change over the years?** → *Line Chart*
3. **What is the composition of profits by segment and subcategory?** → *Stacked Bar Chart*
4. **What is the relationship between discount and sales?** → *Scatter Plot*
5. **Where are returns most frequent and how do they affect profitability?** → *Map + Bar Chart*

✅ *A total of 30 scenario-based visualizations were designed and explained with insights.*

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the [Sample Superstore dataset](https://community.tableau.com/s/question/0D54T00000CWeX8SAL/sample-superstore-sales-excelxls).
2. Open the Tableau workbook (or use your tool of choice).
3. Match each business question with the best visualization type.
4. Refer to `insights/` or `Data_Vizualization_Tools.docx` for the interpretation.

## 💡 Insights

- **Technology** leads in both sales and profit.
- **Furniture** has high sales but underperforms in profitability.
- **West region** drives the most revenue; **South** lags.
- **Standard class** is the most preferred shipping mode.
- **Returns** are most frequent in **California** and the **East Coast**, causing loss in profit.
